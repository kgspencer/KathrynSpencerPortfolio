UNCLOUD

Uncloud is a project made alongside three other interactive computer vision demos as part of the pilot program, Break Points. Installations were developed by individual undergraduate developers and deployed in Clapp Laboratory at Mount Holyoke College.

Uncloud uses MediaPipe, an open-source framework, to detect hand landmarks and reveal an image with real-time hand detection.
